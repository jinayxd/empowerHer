About Us

Equality Forward partners with schools, community groups and local governments to deliver culturally-sensitive gender education, mentor programs and livelihood training. We focus on participatory programs that include boys, girls and non-binary youth to break stereotypes and build shared responsibility.

School Workshops

Youth-led projects

Skills & microgrants

Our approach

  1. Community-led design: programs co-created with local leaders.
  2. Evidence-based: we measure change with pre/post surveys.
  3. Scale through partners: training teachers & youth mentors.

Programs & Impact

Examples of recent projects and outcomes.
